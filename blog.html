How to Use Chat in SciQuest Heroes

The chat feature is one of the most powerful tools in SciQuest Heroes. Here's everything you need to know to help your child get the most out of interactive learning conversations.

What is the Chat Feature?

The chat feature lets your child have interactive conversations with AI character guides about science topics. Instead of just reading or watching, kids can ask questions, get explanations, and explore topics at their own pace. Each topic has its own character guide:

  • Mr. Chloro - Your guide to Photosynthesis
  • Solaris - Explore the Solar System
  • Aqua - Learn about the Water Cycle
  • Dr. Pulse - Discover the Human Body
  • Sparky - Understand Electricity
  • Magno - Explore Magnetism

How to Ask Questions

Asking questions is simple! When your child opens a topic chat, they'll see quick prompt questions they can click, or they can type their own questions. The AI character will respond in a friendly, age-appropriate way that matches your child's grade level.

Example questions:

  • "How do plants make food?"
  • "Why is the sky blue?"
  • "What happens to water when it rains?"
  • "How does my heart work?"

Character Guides

Each character has their own personality and expertise. Mr. Chloro loves talking about plants and photosynthesis. Solaris knows everything about planets and space. These characters make learning feel like a conversation with a friend, not a lecture from a textbook.

Characters respond in character, using language that's appropriate for your child's grade level. A 5-year-old will get simple explanations, while a 12-year-old will get more detailed, scientific answers.

Tips for Getting the Most Out of Chat

  • Encourage curiosity: Let your child ask whatever questions come to mind. There are no wrong questions!
  • Follow up: If your child asks "Why?", encourage them to ask "Why?" again. Deep questions lead to deep understanding.
  • Review together: Check the chat history in your parent dashboard. You can see what your child is curious about and continue the conversation at home.
  • Use quick prompts: The quick prompt questions are great starting points. They're designed to spark curiosity and lead to deeper exploration.
  • Connect to stories: After reading a comic story, encourage your child to chat about it. They can ask questions about what they saw or learned.

Safety and Monitoring

All chat conversations are monitored and reviewable in your parent dashboard. We use AI moderation to ensure all conversations are safe, age-appropriate, and educational. You can review your child's chat history at any time to see what they're learning and curious about.

LEARNING • January 10, 2025

Exploring Science Through Personalized Comics

Personalized comics are at the heart of SciQuest Heroes. Learn how they work, why they're effective, and how to navigate the stories page to find the perfect learning adventure for your child.

How Personalized Comics Work

When your child asks a question or selects a topic, our AI creates a unique comic story where your child is the hero. The story is generated specifically for them, using their name, grade level, and the topic they're curious about. Each story includes:

  • Personalized panels: Your child appears as the main character, exploring science concepts in an engaging visual story.
  • Voice narration: Stories are narrated with your child's name, making the experience feel truly personal.
  • Age-appropriate content: The complexity and language automatically adjust to your child's grade level.
  • Interactive elements: Each panel can be clicked to learn more or start a chat conversation.

Benefits of Story-Driven Learning

Research shows that story-driven learning is more engaging and memorable than traditional methods. When children see themselves as the hero of a story, they:

  • Remember better: Stories create emotional connections that help information stick in memory.
  • Engage more: Personalized content captures attention and maintains interest longer than generic materials.
  • Understand deeper: Visual storytelling helps children understand abstract concepts by making them concrete and relatable.
  • Feel empowered: Being the hero of their own learning story builds confidence and curiosity.

How to Navigate the Stories Page

The Stories page is your child's library of science adventures. Here's how to use it:

  • Browse by topic: Filter stories by science topics like Photosynthesis, Solar System, Water Cycle, and more.
  • View story details: Click on any story to see a preview, read the full story, or download it as a PDF.
  • Launch topic chat: Each story has a "Launch Topic Chat" button that takes your child to an interactive conversation about that topic.
  • Create new stories: Use the comic generator to create new personalized stories based on your child's questions.

Making the Most of Each Story

To maximize learning from each comic story:

  • Read together: Especially for younger children, reading stories together creates bonding time and allows for discussion.
  • Ask follow-up questions: After reading, encourage your child to ask questions in the chat feature.
  • Connect to real life: Help your child see connections between the story and things they observe in daily life.
  • Download and save: PDF downloads let you keep favorite stories and revisit them anytime.
  • Explore related topics: Stories often spark curiosity about related topics. Follow that curiosity!

NGSS Alignment

All our stories are aligned with Next Generation Science Standards (NGSS) for grades K-6. This means your child is learning the same concepts they'll encounter in school, but in a fun, engaging way. You can see which NGSS standards each story covers in the story details.
