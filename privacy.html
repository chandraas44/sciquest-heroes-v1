Policy

Your child's privacy and safety are our top priorities

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

SciQuest Heroes ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of children and their families. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our educational platform designed for children ages 5-12.

We are fully compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and take your child's privacy seriously. By using our service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information from Parents/Guardians

  • Name and email address
  • Payment information (processed securely through third-party payment processors)
  • Account preferences and settings

2.2 Information from Children

  • Child's first name (for personalization)
  • Grade level (to provide age-appropriate content)
  • Avatar selection
  • Stories created and questions asked
  • Chat conversations (monitored and reviewable by parents)
  • Progress and learning data

2.3 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (type, operating system)
  • Usage data (pages visited, features used)
  • IP address (for security and service delivery)

3. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide personalized educational content and stories
  • Adapt content to your child's grade level
  • Enable interactive chat features with AI character guides
  • Track learning progress and achievements
  • Process payments and manage subscriptions
  • Send important service updates (to parent email only)
  • Improve our platform and develop new features
  • Ensure platform security and prevent fraud
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. COPPA Compliance

We are fully compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). This means:

  • We require verifiable parental consent before collecting information from children under 13
  • Parents can review, modify, or delete their child's information at any time
  • We do not share children's information with third parties except as necessary to provide our service
  • We do not use children's information for advertising purposes
  • We maintain reasonable security measures to protect children's information

For more details, please see our COPPA Policy.

5. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or share children's personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party service providers who help us operate our platform (e.g., cloud hosting, payment processing). These providers are contractually obligated to protect information and use it only for specified purposes.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government regulation
  • Safety and Security: To protect the safety, rights, or property of SciQuest Heroes, our users, or others
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (with notice to parents)

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption of data in transit (SSL/TLS)
  • Secure storage of data at rest
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Access controls limiting who can view personal information
  • Secure payment processing through PCI-compliant providers

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Parental Rights and Controls

As a parent or guardian, you have the right to:

  • Review all information collected about your child
  • Request deletion of your child's information
  • Refuse further collection or use of your child's information
  • Revoke consent at any time
  • Access and download your child's data
  • Modify or correct your child's information

To exercise these rights, please contact us at santoshi.atmakuru@gmail.com.

8. Data Retention

We retain your child's information only as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. When you delete an account or request deletion, we will delete or anonymize the information within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal or legitimate business purposes.

9. Third-Party Services

Our platform may use third-party services for:

  • Cloud hosting and data storage
  • Payment processing
  • AI and machine learning services (for story generation and chat)
  • Analytics (aggregated, non-personally identifiable data only)

These services are bound by their own privacy policies and contractual obligations to protect your information.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify parents of any material changes by:

  • Emailing the parent email address on file
  • Posting a notice on our platform
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy

Continued use of our service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your child's information, please contact us:

Email: santoshi.atmakuru@gmail.com
