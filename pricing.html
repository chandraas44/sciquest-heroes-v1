Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

Is it safe for my child? +

Yes! We're COPPA compliant and parents control everything their child sees. No ads, no strangers, no external links. All conversations are monitored and you can review everything in your dashboard. We use industry-standard encryption to protect your child's data, and we never share information with third parties. Your child's safety and privacy are our top priorities.

Can teachers use this in class? +

Absolutely! We have special teacher accounts with class dashboards, lesson plans, and progress tracking. Try it free with your first 30 students. Teachers can create assignments, track student progress, and use our NGSS-aligned content to supplement their curriculum. We offer special pricing for schools and educational institutions.

What age is this for? +

Perfect for kids ages 5-12. Stories automatically adjust to your child's grade level, so content is always age-appropriate and understandable. Our content is aligned with NGSS standards for grades K-6, ensuring that the material matches what your child is learning in school.

How does the free trial work? +

The free trial gives you access to 3 complete stories with all features. No credit card required to start. You can explore our platform, create stories, use the chat feature, and see how your child responds. If you love it, you can upgrade to the monthly membership anytime. If not, you can simply stop using the service - no charges, no hassle.

What's included in the monthly membership? +

The monthly membership includes unlimited stories across all topics, access to all AI characters (Mr. Chloro, Solaris, Aqua, Dr. Pulse, Sparky, Magno), advanced AI chat features, progress tracking, badges and achievements, PDF downloads of stories, and priority support. You get everything we offer with no limits.

Can I cancel anytime? +

Yes! You can cancel your subscription at any time with no penalties or fees. Your membership will remain active until the end of your current billing period, and you'll continue to have access to all features until then. After cancellation, you can still access your account and previously created stories, but you won't be able to create new stories without resubscribing.

Do you offer family plans? +

Currently, our monthly membership covers one child account. If you have multiple children, you can create separate accounts for each child under your parent dashboard. We're working on family plan options that will offer discounts for multiple children. Contact us at santoshi.atmakuru@gmail.com if you're interested in a family plan, and we'll notify you when it becomes available.

Are there teacher discounts? +

Yes! We offer special pricing for teachers and schools. Teacher accounts include class management features, student progress tracking, and lesson plan integration. For school-wide licenses, we offer significant discounts. Please contact us at santoshi.atmakuru@gmail.com with your school information, and we'll provide you with a custom quote.

How do I upgrade from free to paid? +

Upgrading is simple! When you're ready, go to your parent dashboard and click the "Upgrade" button. You'll be prompted to enter your payment information. Once you complete the upgrade, you'll immediately have access to unlimited stories and all premium features. Your billing cycle will start from the day you upgrade.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) and debit cards. We also accept PayPal for your convenience. All payments are processed securely through our payment partners using industry-standard encryption. We never store your full credit card information on our servers.
