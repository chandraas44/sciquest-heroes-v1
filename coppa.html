What is COPPA?

The Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) is a U.S. federal law designed to protect the privacy of children under 13 years of age. COPPA requires websites and online services to obtain verifiable parental consent before collecting, using, or disclosing personal information from children.

SciQuest Heroes is fully compliant with COPPA and takes your child's privacy seriously. This page explains how we comply with COPPA requirements.

Our Commitment

We never collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent. We never sell children's information. We never use children's information for advertising purposes. Parents have full control over their child's data.

1. Parental Consent

Before we collect any personal information from a child under 13, we require verifiable parental consent. We obtain consent through:

  • Requiring parents to create an account and provide their email address
  • Sending a verification email to the parent's email address
  • Requiring parents to confirm their consent before their child can use the Service
  • Maintaining records of parental consent

Parents can revoke consent at any time by contacting us or deleting their child's account.

2. Information We Collect from Children

With parental consent, we collect the following information from children to provide our educational service:

  • First Name: Used to personalize stories and make the experience engaging
  • Grade Level: Ensures content is age-appropriate and matches educational standards
  • Avatar Selection: Allows children to choose how they appear in stories
  • Stories Created: Saves personalized stories for later viewing
  • Questions Asked: Enables the chat feature to provide educational responses
  • Chat Conversations: Monitored and reviewable by parents
  • Progress Data: Tracks learning progress and achievements

We do NOT collect last names, addresses, phone numbers, or any other identifying information beyond what is necessary to provide the Service.

3. How We Use Children's Information

We use children's information solely to:

  • Create personalized educational stories
  • Provide age-appropriate content based on grade level
  • Enable interactive chat features with AI character guides
  • Track learning progress and award achievements
  • Improve our educational platform

We do NOT use children's information for advertising, marketing, or any commercial purposes beyond providing the Service.

4. Information Sharing

We do NOT sell, rent, or share children's personal information with third parties for marketing or commercial purposes. We may share information only:

  • With Service Providers: Trusted companies that help us operate our platform (e.g., cloud hosting, AI services). These providers are contractually required to protect information and use it only for specified purposes.
  • As Required by Law: When required by law, court order, or government regulation
  • For Safety: To protect the safety of children or others

Any third-party service providers we use are carefully vetted and required to comply with COPPA.

5. Parental Rights

Under COPPA, parents have the right to:

  • Review: Request to review all personal information collected about their child
  • Delete: Request deletion of their child's personal information
  • Refuse: Refuse further collection or use of their child's information
  • Revoke Consent: Revoke consent at any time
  • Access: Access and download their child's data
  • Modify: Request corrections to their child's information

To exercise any of these rights, parents can:

  • Use the parent dashboard to manage their child's account
  • Contact us directly at santoshi.atmakuru@gmail.com

6. Security Measures

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect children's information:

  • Encryption of data in transit (SSL/TLS)
  • Secure storage of data at rest
  • Access controls limiting who can view personal information
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Secure authentication for parent accounts
  • Monitoring and logging of access to children's data

7. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain children's information only as long as necessary to provide our Service. When:

  • A parent requests deletion of their child's information
  • An account is deleted or closed
  • Parental consent is revoked

We will delete or anonymize the information within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal or legitimate business purposes (such as maintaining records of consent).

8. No Behavioral Advertising

We do NOT engage in behavioral advertising or targeted advertising to children. Our platform:

  • Does not display third-party advertisements
  • Does not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes
  • Does not share information with advertising networks
  • Does not use children's information to build advertising profiles

9. Contact Information

If you have questions about our COPPA compliance or wish to exercise your parental rights, please contact us:

Email: santoshi.atmakuru@gmail.com

We will respond to all COPPA-related inquiries within 30 days as required by law.
